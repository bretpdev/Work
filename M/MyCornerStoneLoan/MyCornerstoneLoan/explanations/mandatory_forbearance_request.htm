Mandatory Forbearance SERV

This form is used if you are participating in a Medical or Dental Internship/Residency program, a member of  an activated National Guard unit, or participating in the Department of Defense Repayment program and need to postpone your loans.

Before completing this form, read it carefully. As the borrower, you need to complete Sections 1, 2, and 3 of this form. Under Section 2, complete the part that is applicable to your situation.  After you complete the first three portions of the form, an authorized official must complete Section 4 to certify your participation in the applicable program or activated guard unit.
