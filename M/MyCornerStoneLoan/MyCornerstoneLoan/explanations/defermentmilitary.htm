Military Deferment

This form is used for both the Federal Family Education Loan Program and the William D. Ford Federal Direct Loan Program. Read the instructions carefully; you must meet certain criteria to be eligible for this deferment. Complete your portion of the form including your signature and the date. An authorized official such as a commanding officer or personnel officer must complete a section certifying your service dates. When the form is complete submit it to us at the address on the form.

El formulario de Aplazamiento Militar

Este Aplazamiento es para ellos que tienen préstamos en los programas Federal Family Education Loan Program y el William D. Ford Federal Direct loan program. Lee las instrucciones con cuidado, porque para cualificar, usted debe cumplir con ciertos criterios. Antes de enviar el formulario, asegurase que su comandante o un oficial personal han completado la sección verificando su fecha de servicio.
