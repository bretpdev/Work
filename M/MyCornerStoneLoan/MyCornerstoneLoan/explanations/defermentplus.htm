Parent PLUS Deferment Request

Use this form if you are the parent of a student for whom you have taken out a PLUS loan and you wish to defer payments while the student is enrolled in school. Use this form if your loan was taken out after July 1, 2008 or before July 1, 1993. If you took out a loan between those times, please refer to the In-School Deferment Request form for either Direct Loans or Federal Family Education Loan Program loans (there are two separate forms).

When completing the Parent PLUS Borrower Deferment request form, please complete your portions of the form including your signature and the date. An authorized school official must complete a section certifying the enrollment of the student for whom you received the loan. When the form is complete submit it to us at the address on the form.

Solicitud De Aplazamiento de Pago: Padres con Prestamos PLUS

Este aplazamiento se puede usar para padres que pidieron un préstamo PLUS para un estudiante. Si el estudiante está matriculado en la escuela a lo menos de medio tiempo puedes posponer los pagos. Complete este formulario se pidieron los prestamos después el 1 de Julio de 2008 o antes del 1 de julio de 1993. Si pidieron préstamos en medio de las dos fechas por favor elije una de las dos formas (Solicitud de aplazamiento de pago: Estudios) de los programas: Direct Loans o Federal Family Education Loan Program (las dos formas son diferente).

Por favor lea las instrucciones antes de completando el formulario. Un funcionario de la escuela tiene que completar la sección verificando la inscripción escolar. Puede enviar el formulario a la dirección que aparece en el aplazamiento.
