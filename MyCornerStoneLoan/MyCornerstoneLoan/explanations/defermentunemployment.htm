Unemployment Deferment Request

Please select the form for the loan program under which you received your loan: William D. Ford Federal Direct Loan Program, or Federal Family Education Loan Program.

This is the form to choose if you are unemployed or working less than full time and are unable to meet your student loan obligations at this time. Read the instructions and complete the form in its entirety including your signature and the date. When it is complete, return it to us at the address on the form. Don’t forget to include any required documentation.

Solicitud de aplazamiento de pago: Desempleo

Elije el documento que corresponde con el programa que recibió su préstamo educativo: William D. Ford Federal Direct Loan Program, O Federal Family Education Loan Program.

Puede elegir este aplazamiento si te quedaste sin empleo o degastes de trabajar tiempo completo. Por favor lea las instrucciones antes de completar el formulario. Puedes enviar el formulario con los documentos necesarios a la dirección que aparece en el aplazamiento.
