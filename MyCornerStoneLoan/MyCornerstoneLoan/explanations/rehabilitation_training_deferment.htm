Rehabilitation Training Deferment Request

This form is used if you have Direct, FFELP or Perkins loans. Complete this form if you are requesting to postpone payment of your loans while you participate in a Rehabilitation Training program.

Before completing this form, read it carefully. As the borrower, you need to complete Sections 1, 2, and 3 of this form. After you complete the first three portions of the form, an authorized official must complete Section 4 to certify that you are participating in a Rehabilitation Training program.
