Graduate Fellowship Deferment Request

This form is used if you have Direct, FFELP or Perkins loans. Complete this form if you are participating in a Graduate Fellowship program and need to postpone the repayment of your loans.

Before completing this form, read it carefully. As the borrower, you need to complete Sections 1, 2, and 3 of this form. After you complete the first three portions of the form, an authorized official must complete Section 4 to certify your participation in a Graduate Fellowship program.
